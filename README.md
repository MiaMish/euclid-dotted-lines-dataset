# Datasets for the study of dotted lines in early modern editions of *Euclid's Elements* and related works

This repository contains the datasets accompanying the following paper on the use of dotted lines and dots in early modern editions of *Euclid's Elements*. It combines manually curated bibliographical metadata and classifications with links to digitized facsimiles and automated visual-element detections.

> Joskowicz, Mia. 2026. *Dotted Lines and the Reconceptualization of Quantity in Early Modern Editions of Euclid's Elements*. Historia Mathematica. Forthcoming.

## Interactive catalogue

For interactive exploration of the corpus, see:

https://elements-resource-box.netlify.app/catalogue
